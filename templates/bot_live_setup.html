Live trading setup

Run the AI signal bot locally against your own Interactive Brokers account.

  1. Open or clone the open-source Algo-Bot repo on your machine.
  2. Run a scan here and download your config bundle from the results page.
  3. Drop trading_params.json into <algo-bot>/config/.
  4. Copy license.json and license_check.py to the algo-bot root.
  5. At the top of main.py, add: from license_check import verify; verify(strict=True)
  6. Save watchlist.json where your StockTickerFetcher reads from.
  7. Start IB Gateway or TWS in paper mode first — flip to live only when you've verified everything.
  8. From the algo-bot directory: python main.py

Your license, your kill-switch

Every bundle includes a license key tied to your account. The bot phones home every 30 minutes. Cancel your subscription and the next heartbeat returns invalid — the bot exits and stops placing trades. You can also manually revoke or regenerate at any time.

⚠ Risk reminder

We don't custody funds or execute on your behalf. You're responsible for verifying every signal and trade. Test in IB paper mode for at least a few weeks before connecting real money. Finance Buddy is an educational tool, not investment advice.
